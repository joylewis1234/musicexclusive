

# Add Ownership & Control Section to Artist Benefits Page

## What
A new section emphasizing artist ownership, control over revenue, and independence — positioned after the hero (Section 1) and before "Why This Works" (Section 2), so it sets the tone early.

## Section Design

**Title:** "Your Music. Your Money. Your Rules."

Three GlowCards with bold messaging:

1. **Shield icon / "You Own Everything"** — Your masters, your catalog, your decisions. We never take ownership of your music. You stay in control, always.

2. **Lock icon / "Your Revenue, Transparent & Direct"** — See every stream, every cent, every payout in real time. No hidden fees, no mystery math. You know exactly what you earn.

3. **Rocket icon / "Release On Your Terms"** — Drop when you want, pull tracks when you want, set your own preview windows. No gatekeepers, no approval chains.

A closing hype line beneath: *"Most platforms take your leverage. We give it back."*
